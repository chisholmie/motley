Bowl of Soul is made for those cold fall days where all you want is your favorite tea steeped in steamed milk with a touch of honey and foam to top it all off? Fan favorite is the white coconut cream tea with soy milk! Also would recommend trying it with velvet tea!
Everyone knows and loves the Motley’s matcha chacha. Steamed milk, vanilla syrup, and matcha blend together creating a creamy green tea flavor. Take your matcha chacha to the next level by blending in lemonade or white chocolate! Or if you want a more subtle flavor, try adding rose or lavender.
This drink is remarkably similar to a bowl of soul! This drink consists of Earl Grey tea, steamed milk and a pump of vanilla syrup. It’s a perfect pick-me-up for grey winter days.
The Motley offers a lot of twists on Hot Chocolate. Of course their original is amazing, but if you want to try something new, order their Mexican Hot chocolate! It’s got a little more flare and spice than normal hot chocolate. Or if you’re craving something cool, try their blended hot chocolate. It’s the perfect chocolate drink for a warm SoCal day.
(Pro tip: If you’re not ready to try their Mexican Hot Chocolate, try adding a pump of Toasted Marshmallow to your hot chocolate!)
It you’re looking for something to drink after a long day of studying, try out a Motley milk steamer. You can get a milk steamer with any of the flavors the motley has to offer, but fan favorites include rose and lavender. They both bring out subtle flavors that aren’t too sweet or overwhelming.
The Motley has two Chai Latte flavors: spicy and sweet! The chai latte is a classic coffee-free drink consisting of simply steamed milk and chai tea concentrate, but fans recommend spicing it up with some lavender or coconut flavor. Or if you’re looking for a bit more of a caffeine boost, try a Dirty Chai - it comes with a shot of espresso!
When The Motley has their selection of fruits in stock, a smoothie is always the way to go. You can make your own smoothie and fans recommend blending in lemonade (wow what) or a Yerba Mate for an extra boost to get you through those afternoon classes.
This is a normal Mocha but with a white chocolate twist! This drink is a bit sweeter than normal mochas and are a must have for late night Motley runs - blended is highly recommended. Motley goers love adding a pump or two of peppermint or toasted marshmallow to really spice things up.
